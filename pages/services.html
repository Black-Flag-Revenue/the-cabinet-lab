Specialty Service

Vinyl & Thermofoil-Coated Doors

Many kitchens from the 1990s through 2010s have cabinet doors wrapped in vinyl or thermofoil — a heat-bonded plastic film over an MDF core. Over time this wrap peels, bubbles, and looks terrible.

We strip the existing wrap, seal the MDF core, apply primer, and spray with the same 2K catalyzed coating. The result is a solid, durable finish that won't peel — ever.

Always mention vinyl-coated doors when you contact us so we can quote the correct tier and flag any condition issues before drop-off.

Standard Door (vinyl)$100
Drawer Face (vinyl)$75
Pantry / Oversized (vinyl)$120
  • Vinyl/thermofoil stripping included
  • MDF sealing and priming included
  • Full 2K catalyzed spray finish
  • No more peeling — permanent solution

Add-On

Deep Clean Add-On

We sand and prep every door as part of the standard process. But when doors come in with heavy caked-on cooking grease, kitchen exhaust buildup, or significant contamination that goes beyond normal prep, the $15/piece deep clean add-on covers the additional work to get the surface truly ready for coating.

This is not a scrub service for doors you couldn't be bothered to wipe down. It's for genuine heavy contamination — think 20 years of cooking grease on cabinet doors next to the stove.

Assessed At Drop-Off

We'll tell you at drop-off which pieces need the add-on before we start any work. You'll know the total before you leave.
