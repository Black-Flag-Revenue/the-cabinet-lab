Why The Drop-Off Model Changes The Calculation

In-home cabinet painting means a contractor in your kitchen for multiple days — access to your home, fumes, overspray containment, and a schedule you arrange around theirs. Our model is two trips. You load your doors, drive to us, and come back a week later to a finish that a brush-and-roll painter isn't delivering at any price. Most customers who have done it say the drive was the least consequential part of the whole experience.
