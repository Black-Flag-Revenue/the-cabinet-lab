The Process

How It Works

Three simple steps. Professional results. No mess in your home, no contractor scheduling, no markup on materials.

01

Your Job

You Remove Them

This is the part that stays in your home. Take down every cabinet door and drawer face you want refinished. Remove all hardware from each piece — every hinge, every pull, every knob, every handle, every glass insert. If it's attached to the door, it comes off before drop-off.

Give each door a basic wipe-down to remove loose dust and surface debris. You don't need to sand, strip, or deep clean — just make sure they're not caked in grease. If they are, we offer a $15/piece deep clean add-on assessed at drop-off.

Leave the cabinet boxes in your kitchen. We only spray the door panels — the boxes stay in place.

Hardware Removal Is Required

We cannot spray doors with hinges, hardware, or glass attached. If it arrives with hardware, we'll either return it or remove it and add a labor fee. Save yourself the hassle — strip it all before you drop off.

🚪
02

Our Job

We Spray Them

Drop off your doors along with the 2K catalyzed coating you purchased (we'll tell you exactly what to buy — see the Coating Guide). Drop-off payment is $0 — you pay at pickup.

Once in our shop, every door goes through a full professional process:

Inspection

We assess every piece for prep needs — condition, existing finish, vinyl wrap, damage. Any deep-clean add-ons are confirmed here.

Sanding & Surface Prep

Full sanding to scuff the existing surface and create adhesion. We handle all of this — you don't need to sand anything.

Tack & Final Clean

Surfaces are tacked clean immediately before spraying. No dust, no contamination in the finish.

HVLP Spray Application

Applied with a professional turbine HVLP system using your 2K catalyzed coating. Multiple coats, factory-smooth results.

Cure Time

2K coatings cure chemically. We allow proper cure time before release so your finish is hard and ready to handle on pickup.

🔫
03

Back To You

You Reinstall Them

We'll reach out when your doors are ready — typically about a week from drop-off, depending on current workload. Come pick them up, inspect every piece, and pay at pickup. No payment until you're happy with the results.

Rehang your doors, reattach your hardware, and step back. The difference is immediately visible — a glass-smooth, factory-hard finish that looks like brand-new cabinetry at a fraction of replacement cost.

Turnaround Time

Typical turnaround is approximately one week. During busy periods it may be slightly longer — we'll always communicate clearly. Text us at (980) 391-0498 to confirm current availability before dropping off.

Before You Drop Off

Your Complete
Prep Checklist

✓ Do This

  • Remove all doors and drawer faces from your cabinets
  • Remove every hinge, pull, knob, handle and glass insert
  • Wipe surfaces to remove loose dust and light surface debris
  • Purchase the 2K coating we specify (see Coating Guide)
  • Bring the coating with your doors at drop-off
  • Label or organize doors if you have a specific layout plan
  • Note any doors with vinyl/thermofoil wrap when you call/text

✗ We Handle This

  • All sanding and surface preparation
  • Final tack and surface cleaning before spray
  • Primer coats where needed
  • Spray application and coat timing
  • Cure time management
  • Minor surface flaw assessment at drop-off
  • Vinyl wrap removal (on vinyl-coated tier)
