The Story Behind The Spray

Built By A Finisher.
Run By A Finisher.

Cabinet Door Lab isn't a painting company that figured out cabinets. It's a fine finish professional who built a service around doing the one thing they love most.

The Background

Years In The Trade.
A Lifelong Passion
For The Finish.

For years I ran a construction and fine finish company alongside a partner. We did the kind of work that most contractors don't slow down for — the details that separate a job that looks finished from one that actually is. Trim work, millwork, cabinetry, spray finishing. The parts of a project that are invisible when done right, and impossible to ignore when done wrong.

Spray finishing was always where I felt most at home. There's a process to it, a patience, a set of variables that you learn to read and control over time. When everything is dialed in — the equipment, the environment, the material, the timing — the finish that comes out the other side is something you can be genuinely proud of. That's not something you rush.

"A bad hernia and some health challenges eventually forced me out of the heavier side of contracting. But finishing and spraying? That never left."

Developing a bad hernia — one that still gives me trouble — and navigating some kidney health challenges pulled me out of the physical demands of full construction work. The heavy lifting, the extended site days, the physical grind that trades work demands — that chapter had to change.

But the spray gun? That I kept. Cabinet Door Lab is how I stay in the craft in a way that's sustainable — focused, controlled, done in a shop environment where I can do one thing exceptionally well. Every door that comes through here gets sprayed by someone who has spent years caring about this stuff. Not a crew. Not a subcontractor. Me.

The drop-off model is intentional. You do the removal, I do the finishing — the part that actually matters. It keeps costs down for you and lets me focus entirely on the spray work, which is what I'm here to do.

What We Stand For

The Cabinet Door Lab Standard

🎯

One Thing Done Well

We don't paint walls. We don't do whole-house repaints. We spray cabinet doors — that's it. That focus means every system, every process, and every piece of equipment here is optimized for that one job.

🔬

Real Materials

We use catalyzed 2K coatings — the same class of material used by professional cabinet manufacturers. Not hardware store paint. Not "cabinet-specific" latex. The real stuff, applied the right way.

💰

No Markup On Materials

You buy your own coating. You know exactly what went on your doors, you keep the leftover for touch-ups, and you don't pay a mystery contractor markup on the most important part of the job.

✅

Pay At Pickup

You don't pay until you see the finished work. That's not a gimmick — it's how a craftsman should operate. If you're not happy, we talk about it before money changes hands.

📍

Locally Focused

This is a local service for the Hickory, NC area — Statesville, Mooresville, Charlotte, the Lake Norman corridor. Not a franchise. Not a call center. A real person answering your texts and spraying your doors.

🔫

The Equipment Matters

A turbine HVLP system delivers paint at a different atomization level than a rattle can or a brush. The result is a finish that lays flat and smooth — no brush marks, no roller stipple, no lap lines. This is what professional spray finishing looks like.
