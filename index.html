Why Cabinet Door Lab

This Isn't
Paint & Pray.

Most cabinet painters brush or roll. The result looks painted — because it is. We spray every door with a professional turbine HVLP system, the same setup used in production cabinet shops.

The coating is a catalyzed 2K polyurethane — a two-part system that cures chemically to a finish that's harder, smoother, and more durable than anything you can roll on. It resists moisture, grease, cleaning products, and daily kitchen abuse.

Behind Cabinet Door Lab is a finisher with years of fine finish and construction experience — someone who cares about the outcome, not just the throughput.
