How The Drop-Off Model Works For You

Unlike in-home painters, there's no contractor in your house and no scheduling around your household. Load your doors in your vehicle, drive to our Hickory shop, drop them off, and come back about a week later. It's the same trip you'd make to drop off dry cleaning — just with cabinet doors instead. The drive is worth it when the alternative is $3,000+ for a paint-and-pray in-home job.
